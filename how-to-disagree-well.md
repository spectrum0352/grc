Managing the Flow of Conversation
	• Embrace the Pause: Instead of trying to avoid silences 17, suggest taking a moment to consider the topic or coming back to it later18. This gives both parties time to process and respond thoughtfully19.
	• Collaborate on Solutions: Avoid pushing for immediate solutions20. Instead, ask, "What options do you see moving forward?"21. This invites collaboration rather than forcing a specific outcome22.
	• End with Clarity: Don't end a discussion vaguely23. Propose agreeing on next steps and a specific time to check in24. This creates accountability and a clear path forward25.
