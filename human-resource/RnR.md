These are responsibilities not confined to a single role or domain:

- **Zero Trust Implementation**: Enforced by both Cloud and Cybersecurity Architects.

- **SIEM Tools Usage (Splunk, Sentinel)**: Common across Cloud and Cybersecurity Analysts.

- **Security Framework Compliance (CIS, NIST, ISO 27001)**: Handled by both Cloud and Cybersecurity Consultants.

- **Incident Response & Vulnerability Management**: Shared by Analysts and Engineers across domains.

- **EDR/XDR & Endpoint Hardening**: Managed by Engineers (Cloud and Cybersecurity).

These tasks can fall under different roles depending on the organization or seniority:

- **ServiceNow Ticket Management**: Performed by Analysts, Engineers, and Consultants.

- **Security Team Collaboration (e.g., NSG misconfigurations)**: Involves Engineers, Analysts, and Architects.

- **Risk Assessments & Recommendations**: Core to Consultants but also relevant to senior Analysts and Architects.

- **Security Architecture Evolution**: Involves Architects and Consultants in both cloud and traditional security.
